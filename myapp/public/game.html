Rules:

  • There are 8 different coloured pins. One player makes a code out of 4 pins, the order also matters!. Colours can be used multiple times.
  • The other player has 10 guesses to crack the code. The player chooses 4 pins and places them on the board in the order they want.
  • After submitting a guess, the player that made the code has to rate the guess, they do this by rating each individual pin. A pin can be rated in 3 ways:
    • Green: The pin is of the right colour, and in the right place.
    • Yellow: The pin is of the right colour, but not in the right place.
    • Red: This colour pin is not in the code.
  • The guesser then gets back these 4 ratings, however, the guesser does not know which rating belongs to which pin. So all the guesser knows, is that they had x pins that were the right colour and in the right spot, y pins that were the right colour, but in the wrong spot, and z pins that were not in the combination at all.
  • If the guesser can crack the code within 10 guesses, they win, otherwise, the code-maker wins!
  • Click (and hold) on the logo to the right for a surprise!
